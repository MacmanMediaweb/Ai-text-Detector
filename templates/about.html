Understanding AI Text Detection Technology

How it works

This system uses a hybrid approach to detect AI-generated text by combining two powerful methodologies:

Stylometric Analysis

Analyzes linguistic patterns, sentence structure, and vocabulary richness to identify human-like vs. machine-like writing signatures.

Transformer Model

Uses deep learning patterns to recognize the underlying statistical structures typical of modern Large Language Models (LLMs).

Our Mission

In an era of rapidly evolving AI, our goal is to provide transparency and tools that help distinguish between human creativity and synthetic content, promoting authenticity across the digital landscape.
